Who is Dirk

I have been a journalist for over 10 years, writing for a lot of different media. I worked for newspapers, radio and television. I learned to write articles and developed other skills such as camera techniques, photography and video editing. Today journalists are required to have a certain technical expertise. I embraced the possibilities and loved learning new skills.

Which brings me to my new field. Two years ago I became interested in software development. I enrolled in a few courses and ever since, I've become more interested. Being able to create my own website without Wordpress or Joomla, is thrilling. But the possibilities are endless. I can even create my own app or setup a database.

I believe journalism and software development share a lot of similarities. Of course, they are two very different jobs, but they both require some creativity and punctuality.

To keep myself balanced, I enjoy jogging and a good film.
